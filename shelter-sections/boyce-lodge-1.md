LT Mile 134.6 — Division 7 — Elev. 2930 ft

Mentioned in O'Kane, 1926, it was located probably less than 112 miles from the present shelter. Blowdown Camp is also mentioned in O'Kane which may be another name for Boyce Lodge. Erected in 1925-26, built of unpeeled logs, open front with bunks for 12 or 15 persons, stove and simple cooking utensils, good water. To be completed by August 1, 1926. [GB 6th Edition 1924]
