Mile 0.6 North Shore Tr. — Division 3 — Elev. 2555 ft

This is an open front log shelter, with bunks for 6 or 8, erected by the Worcester Section in 1938. [GB 12th Edition 1940] This shelter was in use in 1985. This log structure with space for 6 was built in 1937 by the Worcester section and is aptly named having a gorgeous view of Stratton Mt. from a beautiful lake shore. [GB 23rd Edition 1985] No caretaker at Stratton Pond. [1955 Supplement to GB 14th Edition 1951]
