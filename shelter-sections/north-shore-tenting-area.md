Mile 0.2 on North Shore Tr. — Division 3 — Elev. 2555 ft

This tenting area is located .5 mile from the LT on Stratton Pond and is near the site of the Stratton View Shelter which was dismantled after 1986. It has tent platforms. The Worcester Section has developed a primitive camping area on land owned by Gardiner Lane at Stratton Pond. Gardiner offered this for a pilot project as a possible solution for the rapidly increasing use of the LT. [LTN August 1972]
