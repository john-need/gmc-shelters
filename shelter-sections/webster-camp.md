LT Mile 39.2 — Bennington Section — Elev. 2300 ft

At the Grout Job is Camp Webster which is leased and furnished by some of the Bennington section members. [GB 4th Edition 1922] At the Grout Job a new Webster Camp will be built which will be the second camp in this location. [GB 6th Edition 1924] No longer mentioned as a place for shelter in GB 9th Edition 1932.

Camp Webster near present day Daniel Webster monument on Arlington Wardsboro Road. Here Webster spoke to 15000 people in 1840 at a Whig Convention.
