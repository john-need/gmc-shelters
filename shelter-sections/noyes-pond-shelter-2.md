LT Mile 111.3 Killington Section — Elev. 2.360 ft

The lodge with accommodations for 20 persons is set on a bluff about 50 feet above and 100 yards back from pond. [GB 6th Edition 1924] Open front shelter, built in 1923, simple cooking utensils and bunks for 10, is set on the slope about 100yards back: from the pond. [GB 11th Edition 1937]
