Continuing north along the ridge the Trail then crops rapidly in the midst of beautiful cliffs and arrives at Theron Dean Lodge, built n 1924, a shelter of Swiss type with bunks for four. Splendid views of mountains to the north but water supply fails in dry weather [O'Kane, 1926] This shelter can be used in an emergency [GB 14th Edition 1951] The 17th Edition of the GB describes this shelter as abandoned in 1963.
