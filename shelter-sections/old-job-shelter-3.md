Mile 61.9 LT — Division 4 — Elev. 1650 ft

See Griffith Camp for 1st & 2nd.

This log structure with space for 8 was built in 1935 by the Civilian Conservation Corps Lake Brook provides water. Its location is near where the Village of Griffith once stood. [GB 17th Edition 1963] It was at the edge of a clearing where sawdust piles remain in 1963 Its location may be the same as the present shelter built in 1963 It was used from 1935 to 1962.
