Mile 4.8 Branch Pond Tr. — Division 3 — Elev. 2540 ft

The frame lean-to, with bunks for 6 to 8, was built by the USFS in 1965. A path leads to the pond where there is a good view of Stratton Mountain. [GB 22nd Edition 1983] The shelter was dismantled and removed by the USFS in 1983. [LTN Feb. 1984]
