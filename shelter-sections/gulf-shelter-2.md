Mile 14.5 — Division AT 1

This log lean-to with bunks for 6 was originally constructed on a site 0.1 mile from its new site. In 1977 it was moved to the new location southwest of its former site and a wood floor was added. It was in use at this location from 1977 to 1983 when it was again moved to another location by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and renamed the Wintturi Shelter. [GB 21st 1977 & 23rd 1985 eds.]
