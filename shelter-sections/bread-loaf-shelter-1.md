LT Mile 131.4 — Division 7 — Elev. 3300 ft

A log structure built by the Lake Pleiad Section in 1931 has bunks for about 10. Brook water adjacent. In use from 1931 to 1960 when the Emily Proctor Shelter was constructed. [GB 50th Anniversary Edition 1960 (16th)] The 29-year-old shelter was taken. down. [LT News, Nov. 1960]
