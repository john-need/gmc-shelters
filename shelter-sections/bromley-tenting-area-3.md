Mile 18.2 — Division 3 — Elev. 2080 ft

Two tent platforms are located here. A communal fireplace and outhouse are located here. It is the former site of Bromley Camp which was removed in 1984. In use from 1985 to present. [GB 24th Edition_ 1996] The GMC is investigating a new site for this tenting area. [LTN Fall 1997]
