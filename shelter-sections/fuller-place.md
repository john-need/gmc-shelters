Herb Haley Farm  
Mile 11.5 — Division 5 [GB 23rd Edition 1985]

A short distance north of the Upper Road, on a branch of the Cold River, board and lodging could be obtained by hikers. [O'Kane; 1926] The GMC 1920 "Guidebook of the Long Trail describes this farm as providing good beds, board and a telephone for trail users. A clearing marks the site of this farm.
