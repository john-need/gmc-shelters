LT Mile 107.3 — Division 6 — Elev. 2300 ft

This camp located 300 ft back from Noyes Pond, is of frame construction with bunks for about 8. Spring water is just in front of the camp. It was built in 1932 and is privately owned and maintained by agreement it is open to hikers, except during the deer hunting season in November when it is occupied by its owners or locked [GB 17th Edition 1963] Both of these camps were in use for a period of time prior to 1951.
