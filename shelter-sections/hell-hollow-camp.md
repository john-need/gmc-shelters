This camp was located near the location of the more recent Fay Fuller Camp at the end of a road about 1-5 miles from the present-day Route 9. It was in a valley known as Hell Hollow. This camp is a closed cabin and is equipped. The key was obtainable at the Griswold's store in Bennington. [O'Kane; 1926] Mentioned in GB 1st Edition 1917 as fully equipped with bunks for 10 persons. This camp was not mentioned in GB 8th Edition 1930.
