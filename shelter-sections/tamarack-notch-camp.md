This camp is operated by the Grace Congregational Church of Rutland in the Aitken State Forest in Mendon near the LT. The camp is the site of the GMC 1977 lntersectional. It is available to large and small groups for $3. to 6. per day. It offers cabins, shelters and campsites and can be reached by taking the Bucklin Trail from Cooper Lodge to the Wheelerville Road, tum left and go 3 miles to the Notch Road. [LTN May 1979]
