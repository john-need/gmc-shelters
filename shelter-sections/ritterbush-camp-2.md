Mile 19.0 — Division 11 — Elev. 1200 ft

This camp is a frame cabin with space for 8 and was built in 1933 by the LT Patrol. A brook 200 ft north on the LT furnishes water. It has been in use from 1933 until March of 1999 when it was burned. [Sterling Newsletter. April 1999 & GB 24th Edition 1996] Removal was completed in June 1999 by Scott Christiansen, Pete Ketcham, and volunteers from the Vermont Student Assistance Corp. on a very hot buggy day. [LTN Fall 1999]
