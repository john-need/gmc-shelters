Mile 2.8 — Division 1 — Elev. 2200 ft

On a 300 yd spur trail west from the LT. frame lean to was built in 1965 by carpenter trainees of the Manpower Development Training Act It has space for 8. A brook, which may fail in cry seasons, is 150 yds. to the west. A primitive tenting area is located 400 ft south of the shelter on a spur trail. It was in use from 1965 to May 1971.
